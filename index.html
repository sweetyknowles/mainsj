Core Services


Product Strategy & Technical Ownership (Product Owner)

Define product vision, multi-release roadmaps, and PI planning aligned with business strategy and technical feasibility. Prioritize backlog items using value, complexity, dependencies, and risk analysis to ensure delivery of high-impact features. Translate strategic objectives into epics, user stories, and technical acceptance criteria that are actionable for engineering teams. Collaborate with architects, engineers, and DevOps teams to validate solution feasibility, integrations, scalability, and maintainability. Support prototyping, spike solutions, and technical validation to reduce delivery risk and accelerate feedback loops. Manage feature dependencies and release planning across multiple teams in scaled agile frameworks (SAFe, LeSS, or hybrid).

Business Analysis & Technical Requirements (Business Analyst)

Conduct end-to-end requirements discovery and analysis: process flows, data models, system interaction diagrams, and user journeys. Translate business needs into functional and technical specifications that are actionable for developers and testers. Assess technical dependencies, APIs, integrations, and infrastructure constraints to ensure feasibility and identify potential risks. Define acceptance criteria, test scenarios, and traceability matrices linking requirements to delivered features and operational outcomes. Facilitate workshops with stakeholders to clarify business rules, edge cases, workflows, and system behaviors. Maintain requirements artifacts throughout the lifecycle, ensuring clarity, version control, and audit readiness. Provide actionable insights on design trade-offs, scalability, and operational impact, bridging the gap between business objectives and technical implementation.

Agile Delivery Leadership (Scrum Master / Agile Delivery Lead)

Facilitate agile ceremonies: sprint planning, backlog refinement, reviews, retrospectives, and daily stand-ups. Coordinate cross-team dependencies, risks, and impediments to maintain delivery predictability. Track and report metrics such as velocity, cycle time, defect trends, technical debt, and delivery predictability. Support scaled agile practices, including PI planning, iteration tracking, and release coordination across multiple teams. Coach teams and stakeholders on agile best practices, lean product thinking, and continuous improvement. Ensure transparency between technical teams and leadership, providing actionable insights for decision-making and risk mitigation.

Technical & Product Documentation

Produce structured user stories, functional and technical specifications, API references, system guides, and release notes. Ensure documentation supports traceability, operational adoption, and knowledge transfer. Leverage modern tooling and AI-enabled workflows to streamline documentation without compromising accuracy or compliance.
